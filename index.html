About Us

Building Value Through Disciplined Investment

Learn about our company's mission, history, and approach to investment management.

Kapital LLC is an investment management firm serving as the General Partner for S Fund, LP - an Investment Partnership based in Texas and established in 2022.

The fund uses a value investing approach aided by advanced quantitative models. We seek to identify and invest in the best opportunities amongst a broad number of investable securities and are focused on relatively liquid securities.

Our Philosophy

The Disciplined Application of Fundamental Analysis

Our investment approach is guided by time-tested principles and enhanced by cutting-edge quantitative tools.

Value Investing

Value investing is the disciplined application of fundamental analysis to identify potentially undervalued businesses and securities.

  • Identify undervalued assets
  • Focus on long-term value creation
  • Fundamental data analysis

Quantitative Models

We employ advanced quantitative models to analyze large datasets, identify patterns, and enhance our fundamental research process.

  • Run back-tests on historical data
  • Advanced pattern recognition
  • Risk-adjusted return optimization

Investment Realization

Profits can be derived when and if their value is subsequently recognized by other market participants.

  • Disciplined execution
  • Strategic exit timing
  • Capital redeployment
